What is Drawster?

Drawster is a very simple image creator. Drawster has many features and even some that most (image) editors don't have.

←←Whats new in version 0.1.7.0?←←

  • Erkspace:
    • Upload an erkspace to continue where you left off
    • Dowload an erkspace to save your workspace so you can finish later
  • Add titles to your pictures

←←Whats new in version 0.1.8.0?←←

  • Less lag when drawing
  • Erkspace improvements
  • Pen mode updated. pen mode is now much, MUCH better than the old pen.
  • The old pen mode is now circle mode
  • Circle mode is deprecated. it will be removed in future versions.
  • <Version 0.1.8.1:
    • click the space bar for new drawing settings
    • on the bottom of the controls you now have a selector to choose what kind of computer you have.
    • if you click the awesome computer button then you do not have to click the update links button.

A fast, yet simple image creator

Drawster is a simple image creator that comes packed with tons of features. Drawster gets updates frequently and still remains easy to use. there is no dowload needed with Drawster, so you can skip the wait and start creating right away! Drawster can be used for a variety of different things (letter, images, etc.) Drawster is safe and stable.
